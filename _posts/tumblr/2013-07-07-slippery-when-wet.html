Two products that work hard to keep you from using them:

  1. Shampoo Bottles with horrible tiny nubs that are meant to be used to open the shampoo bottles, but don’t work when hands are wet & sudsy.
  2. Packets of Ketchup inevitably made slippery and impossible to open by junkfood-greased hands.
