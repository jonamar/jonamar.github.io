Making a lot of tea for myself today and it got me daydreaming about the perfect kettle. And that reminded me about the old adage, put here nicely by Paul Rand (1914 - 1996) a brilliant American Graphic Designer:

Ideas may also grow out of the problem itself, which in turn becomes part of the solution.

Design, in so many ways, is about problem solving and design thinking is about getting to know the problem you’re solving, intimately.
