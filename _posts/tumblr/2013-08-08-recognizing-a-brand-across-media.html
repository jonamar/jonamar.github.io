I recently fell across a brilliant new CMS called Craft. It seems to be the breath of fresh air I’ve been looking for… at least, one of them. It’s made by what I assume is a small team and they’re doing a tremendous job putting together all their documentation, communities, etc. There’s a lot going on and the real job of running a company comes down to prioritizing. So, I’m guessing they’ve probably actually made the right decision in not paying attention to the detail I’m about to bring up…

Enough Flattery, Here’s my Gripe

We come into contact with a brand in a lot of places. The locations are familiar: Website, Twitter, Google+, Facebook, Github, et al. Modern brands aren’t traditional corner groceries — same location, same signage, same aisles — but our brains are the same and they need something to hitch some neurons to, we need to make our brands recognizable and that means one big thing: consistency.
