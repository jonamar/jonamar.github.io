Shmidt Sergey - http://shmidt.in/

So simple. Just a stack of images, intermingling graphic design and photography, paired with short descriptions. His work speaks for itself though (see two images below).

Vaclav Krbusek - http://vaclavkrbusek.com/

This one was my favourite. And that’s saying a lot given that it’s a full flash-based website, which for me, is typically a bad thing. But the experience this site delivers is so fun, funny, fresh, and clear that I don’t care how he pulled it off. The little details like how his voice gets quieter if you scroll away from his talking head just tickle me pink.

A great lesson in the fact that its the results more than the tools that matter (but of course, part of the results of this tool choice is that he’s limiting the full experience to flash users). 

Stephen Burgess - http://minimalmonkey.com/

Such a delightful website. It’s all in the details here with bouncing icons and fading panels. So original yet completely intuitive. And that’s not by accident this Design uses flair for a purpose, not just to delight, but to lead.
