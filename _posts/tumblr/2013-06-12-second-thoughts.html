Technology, the web, has us all underneath this collective microscope of self-awareness. We often remark, in some combination of horror & jest, that we’re wasting our lives on cat gifs and youtube memes.

Fast Company recently published a great article exploring the view from the microscope stage back up. They problematize our assumptions that these memes are mindless and that sharing them is even less so. I found this paragraph particularly poignant (alliteration hat-trick!)

In the language of the visual web, when we share a video or an image, we’re not just sharing the object, we’re also sharing in the emotional response it creates.
